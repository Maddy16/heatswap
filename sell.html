why use heatswap?

you save money

When you open up a store on HeatSwap you take home more money. We charge 8% per sale compared to 10-12% charged by eBay and Amazon.

we help market your shoes

Everyday we pick out select shoes on the site and show them off on Facebook, Instagram, and on YouTube. Even if your shoes are not among those featured you'll benefit from our promotional efforts.

we're sneakerheads – like you

Since we're Sneakerheads ourselves we understand what you want – a safe and secure way to buy and sell sneakers. Toward that end we make it easy to give feedback and allow payment through trusted sources. Plus our customer service team is on standby to help make the experience a good one for you.

you get to help kids

We've pledged to throw some heat to communities who need a little help. We give one percent of every sale to support healthy play and educational opportunities for kids who could use a break. Please join us.
